Picking just one favoite romance movie is a challenge. But I feel I may have narrowed it down to just three... There are so many more but there isn't enough time for them all on one little page.

  • Practical Magic
  • Star Dust
  • The Princess Bride

These are my top personal favorite movies, and I feel many could agree. These movies are classic and hold more than the mushy lovey dovey stuff. They contain action scenes, comedy and drama and of course... love!

They wouldn't be "romance movies" without it! I feel they have a little something for the whole family to enjoy, or for one individual who just wants to curl up and enjoy some time spent being taken away to a new world.
